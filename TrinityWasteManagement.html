Trinity College Waste Management Program

We have started a waste management program where you can upload an image of the waste and tell us where it is located. Then we will relay this information to Doko Recyclers, Solid Waste Management Association Nepal (SWMAN) and Trinity College. Every weekend we go out and collect the waste to dispose of it.
